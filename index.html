About Us

Manufacturing sector will continue to remain one of the biggest demand drivers in emerging market of India.The demand for warehousing space is ever increasing.

Mumbai is the commercial capital and Bhiwandi is in close proximity to large consumption markets of Mumbai,Thane along with Navi Mumbai.

We offer complete logistics solution at most competitive rates.

Our Services

Transport

In the wake of rapid urbanization movement of goods in the city limits has increased multi-fold. We do our best to cater whooping demand and provide our goods transportation services to manufacturing industry.

We provide End to End Logistic Solution.Our transport division offers mobility solutions for sensible network between Consignors and their dealers. As per company requirements we provide FTL/LTL service.

Goods delivered within 24 hours. Special dedicated vehicles on daily/monthly basis can be arranged.

We have been serving across all categories from Medicines to Foods , FMCG Goods to Home Appliances.We serve industry leaders and act as their reliable distribution network partners.

Warehousing

Warehousing play critical role as inventory buffering points.It is also a very critical factor of supply chain management. We intend to reduce service response time and efficiently process the inventory.

We provide fully functional warehousing facility as per requirements. Computerised records management, material management, update and clear stock are our specialities.we provide budget friendly and flexible warehousing options for business storage space requirements.

We are located in the URBANISED BSNA (Bhiwandi Surrounding Notified Area), easily accessible from all cargo transit routes like those of Nasik, Agra, Ahmedabad, Goa and Mumbai.The warehouse is secured with high quality CCTV for 24x7 surveillance for maintaining the safety and security of the facility.

Freight Forwarding

Freight forwarding service guarantee's that all goods reach to desired destination on agreed date in good condition. We have nurtured good relationships with all the established carriers.

Through freight forwarding service we do negotiate best possible price to move goods along most economical route. For deriving most economical route we work on various bids and choosing the one best on matix of reliability, speed, and cost.

We provide Clearing & Forwarding services.C&F agents basically known as Third Party Logistics (3PL).They are gaining importance at a rapid pace in logistics business environment. It helps organizations in cost cutting and stay one step ahead in market. It also improves the "Turn Around Time to Market" which plays a significant role in customer satisfaction.
